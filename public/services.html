Before you ask

Quick Answers.
No Runaround.

How quickly can you come out?

In a genuine emergency we're usually on-site within the hour across Cardiff, and same-day everywhere else in South Wales. For booked jobs, same-week is standard — often sooner.

Do you give fixed quotes before starting?

Yes. Every job gets a written, fixed quote upfront — no call-out surprises, no sliding hourly rates. If the scope changes mid-job (rare), we stop and agree it with you before carrying on.

What if something goes wrong after you've left?

Workmanship is covered for 12 months on every installation. If it's our install and something fails, we come back and fix it — no charge. Manufacturer warranties run alongside that on new parts and boilers.

Do you cover my area?

We cover all of South Wales — Cardiff, Penarth, Barry, Caerphilly, Bridgend, Newport and the Valleys. Free quotes are standard within Cardiff. Give us a call if you're not sure.
