Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Fired by Purpose

Warming Us was founded in 2020 with a single, clear ambition — to bring reliable, honest, and expert heating services to homes and businesses across Cardiff. Born from a genuine passion for doing the job properly, we set out to be the kind of tradespeople people actually want to call.

From the very beginning, we've been fully insured and committed to the highest standards of workmanship. Every boiler we fit, every pipe we run, every heating system we commission is treated as if it were in our own home. That standard has never changed — and it never will.

We work across Cardiff and the surrounding areas, building lasting relationships with our customers one warm home at a time. Whether it's a routine service, an emergency repair, or a full system upgrade, you'll always get the same dedicated, professional team.
